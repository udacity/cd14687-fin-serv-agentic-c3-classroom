ATM Network & Reimbursement Rules (2025)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. ATM NETWORK OVERVIEW
1.1 Partner Networks:
    - MoneyPass Network: 40,000+ ATMs nationwide
    - Allpoint Network: 55,000+ ATMs worldwide
    - CO-OP Network: 30,000+ ATMs (credit union partnership)

1.2 Fee-Free ATM Locations:
    - All bank-owned ATMs (500+ locations)
    - Partner network ATMs (see section 1.1)
    - Select retail locations (7-Eleven, CVS, Walgreens)

2. ATM FEE REIMBURSEMENT PROGRAMS
2.1 Premier Checking Account:
    - Up to 6 out-of-network ATM fee reimbursements per month
    - Includes both bank fees and surcharge fees
    - Domestic ATMs only (excludes international)
    - Automatic credit within 2 business days

2.2 Business Elite Checking:
    - Unlimited domestic ATM fee reimbursements
    - Up to $25 per month in international ATM fee reimbursements
    - Real-time reimbursement for partner network fees

2.3 Standard Checking Accounts:
    - No ATM fee reimbursements
    - $2.50 fee per out-of-network transaction

3. SURCHARGE REFUND POLICY
3.1 Eligible Fees:
    - Third-party ATM operator surcharges
    - Out-of-network usage fees charged by our bank
    - Balance inquiry fees at non-partner ATMs

3.2 Non-Eligible Fees:
    - International conversion fees
    - ATM cash advance fees (credit cards)
    - Fees for declined transactions due to insufficient funds

4. INTERNATIONAL ATM USAGE
4.1 Standard Fees:
    - Out-of-network fee: $5.00 per transaction
    - Foreign transaction fee: 3% of transaction amount
    - Local ATM operator fees apply

4.2 Premier Account Benefits:
    - Reduced foreign transaction fee: 1% of transaction amount
    - $25 monthly cap on international ATM fees (Business Elite only)

5. LIMITS AND RESTRICTIONS
5.1 Daily ATM Withdrawal Limits:
    - Standard Checking: $500 per day
    - Premier Checking: $1,000 per day
    - Business accounts: $2,500 per day (or as agreed)

5.2 Reimbursement Processing:
    - Reimbursements processed on business days only
    - Credits appear within 2 business days of transaction
    - Monthly reimbursement limit resets on statement cycle date
