Domestic & International Wire Transfer Policy (2025)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. DOMESTIC WIRE TRANSFERS
1.1 Same-Day Processing Cut-off Times:
    - Standard Business Days: 3:00 PM ET
    - Fridays: 2:30 PM ET
    - Federal Holidays: No same-day processing

1.2 Domestic Wire Fees:
    - Outgoing: $25.00 per wire
    - Incoming: $15.00 per wire
    - Rush Processing (after cut-off): $50.00 additional

1.3 Next Business Day Processing:
    - Cut-off: 5:00 PM ET on business days
    - Fee: Same as same-day processing

2. INTERNATIONAL WIRE TRANSFERS
2.1 Processing Times:
    - Standard: 1-3 business days
    - Cut-off for same-day initiation: 2:00 PM ET

2.2 International Wire Fees:
    - Outgoing: $45.00 per wire
    - Incoming: $25.00 per wire
    - Correspondent bank fees may apply

2.3 Currency Conversion:
    - Exchange rates updated hourly during business hours
    - Margin applied: 2.5% above interbank rate

3. LIMITS AND REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Daily Limits:
    - Personal accounts: $100,000 per day
    - Business accounts: $250,000 per day (or as agreed)

3.2 Required Information:
    - Beneficiary name and address
    - Receiving bank routing number (domestic) or SWIFT code (international)
    - Account number
    - Purpose of transfer (for international wires)

4. SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
4.1 Identity Verification Required for:
    - All wires over $3,000
    - First-time beneficiaries
    - International transfers to high-risk countries

4.2 Processing may be delayed for:
    - OFAC screening
    - Anti-money laundering review
    - Incomplete information
