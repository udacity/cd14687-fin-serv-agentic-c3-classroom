HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFITS POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document Version: 2025.1

COVERAGE OVERVIEW

All full-time employees are eligible for comprehensive health insurance coverage beginning on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment. Part-time employees working 20+ hours per week are eligible after 60 days.

PLAN OPTIONS

Premium Plan
- Monthly Premium: $150 (employee), $450 (family)
- Annual Deductible: $500 individual, $1,500 family
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $3,000 individual, $9,000 family
- Copay: $20 primary care, $40 specialist
- Prescription Coverage: $10 generic, $30 brand, $50 specialty
- Coverage: 90% after deductible

Standard Plan
- Monthly Premium: $75 (employee), $250 (family)
- Annual Deductible: $1,500 individual, $4,500 family  
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $6,000 individual, $18,000 family
- Copay: $30 primary care, $60 specialist
- Prescription Coverage: $15 generic, $40 brand, $70 specialty
- Coverage: 80% after deductible

Basic Plan
- Monthly Premium: $25 (employee), $150 (family)
- Annual Deductible: $3,000 individual, $9,000 family
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $8,000 individual, $24,000 family
- Copay: $50 primary care, $100 specialist
- Prescription Coverage: $25 generic, $60 brand, $100 specialty
- Coverage: 70% after deductible

ENROLLMENT PERIODS

Open Enrollment: November 1-30 annually
Changes effective: Following January 1
Qualifying Life Events: 30-day window from event date
- Marriage or divorce
- Birth or adoption
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in employment status

DEPENDENT COVERAGE

Eligible dependents include:
- Legal spouse or domestic partner
- Children up to age 26
- Disabled dependents (no age limit with proper documentation)

WELLNESS BENEFITS

- Annual physical exam: 100% covered (no deductible)
- Preventive screenings: 100% covered
- Flu shots: 100% covered
- Health coaching: Available at no cost
- Gym membership reimbursement: Up to $50/month with Premium Plan

DENTAL AND VISION

Included in all plans at no additional cost:
- 2 dental cleanings per year (100% covered)
- 1 comprehensive eye exam per year
- $200 allowance for glasses or contacts annually

CLAIMS AND REIMBURSEMENT

Submit claims within 90 days of service date through online portal or mobile app. Reimbursement processed within 14 business days for approved claims. Out-of-network services reimbursed at 60% of in-network rates.
