401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document Version: 2025.1

ELIGIBILITY

All employees age 21 or older are eligible to participate in the company 401(k) plan immediately upon hire. Enrollment can begin on the first day of employment.

EMPLOYEE CONTRIBUTIONS

Contribution Limits (2025):
- Under age 50: Maximum $23,000 per year
- Age 50+: Maximum $30,500 per year (includes $7,500 catch-up)

Contribution Options:
- Traditional (pre-tax): Reduces current taxable income
- Roth (after-tax): Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
- Split contributions allowed between both types

Payroll Deductions:
- Minimum: 1% of gross salary
- Maximum: 75% of gross salary (IRS limit)
- Changes: Can update anytime, effective next pay period

COMPANY MATCH

Standard Match Formula:
- 100% match on first 3% of salary contributed
- 50% match on next 2% of salary contributed
- Maximum company contribution: 4% of salary

Example Scenarios:
Employee contributes 1%: Company matches 1% (total 2%)
Employee contributes 3%: Company matches 3% (total 6%)
Employee contributes 5%: Company matches 4% (total 9%)
Employee contributes 10%: Company matches 4% (total 14%)

Match Timing:
- Contributed with each paycheck
- Subject to vesting schedule

VESTING SCHEDULE

Graded Vesting for Company Match:
- Year 1: 0% vested
- Year 2: 25% vested
- Year 3: 50% vested
- Year 4: 75% vested
- Year 5+: 100% vested

Note: Employee contributions are always 100% vested immediately

INVESTMENT OPTIONS

Target Date Funds: 10 options (2030-2070)
- Automatically adjust allocation as retirement approaches
- Low expense ratios: 0.10-0.15%

Index Funds:
- S&P 500 Index
- Total Stock Market Index
- International Stock Index
- Bond Index
- Real Estate Index

Managed Funds:
- Growth Fund
- Value Fund
- Balanced Fund
- Income Fund

Company Stock:
- Limited to maximum 20% of portfolio
- Subject to trading windows and blackout periods

LOANS AND HARDSHIP WITHDRAWALS

401(k) Loans:
- Available after 1 year of participation
- Borrow up to 50% of vested balance or $50,000 (whichever is less)
- Interest rate: Prime + 1%
- Repayment period: 5 years (15 years for home purchase)
- Repayments via payroll deduction

Hardship Withdrawals:
- Must demonstrate immediate financial need
- Options: medical expenses, home purchase, tuition, foreclosure prevention
- Subject to 10% early withdrawal penalty if under age 59.5
- Regular income tax applies

AUTOMATIC FEATURES

Auto-Enrollment:
- New hires automatically enrolled at 3% contribution
- Can opt out within first 30 days
- Annual auto-increase of 1% up to 10%

Auto-Rebalancing:
- Portfolio rebalanced quarterly to maintain target allocation
- Can disable if preferred

DISTRIBUTION OPTIONS

Retirement Age Withdrawals (59.5+):
- Lump sum distribution
- Periodic payments
- Annuity options
- Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs) begin at age 73

Early Withdrawal:
- 10% penalty plus income tax (before 59.5)
- Exceptions: disability, certain medical expenses, first home purchase

Separation from Company:
- Leave funds in plan (if balance > $5,000)
- Roll over to IRA or new employer plan
- Take distribution (subject to taxes/penalties)

FEES AND EXPENSES

Plan Administration Fee: $50 per year per participant
Investment Fund Fees: 0.10% - 0.85% depending on fund
No transaction fees for trades or rebalancing
No loan origination fees

BENEFICIARY DESIGNATION

Must designate primary and contingent beneficiaries
Spousal consent required for non-spouse primary beneficiary
Update beneficiaries after major life events
