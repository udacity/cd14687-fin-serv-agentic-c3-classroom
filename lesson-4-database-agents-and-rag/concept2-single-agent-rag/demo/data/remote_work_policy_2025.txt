REMOTE WORK AND FLEXIBLE SCHEDULE POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document Version: 2025.1

REMOTE WORK OPTIONS

Hybrid Schedule (Most Common):
- In-office: 2-3 days per week (manager discretion)
- Remote: 2-3 days per week
- Core collaboration days: Tuesday and Thursday (in-office preferred)
- Flexible days: Monday, Wednesday, Friday

Full Remote:
- Available for eligible roles after 6 months employment
- Requires manager and department head approval
- Must reside in approved states
- Annual in-person meetings required (4 times per year)

Occasional Remote:
- All employees may work remotely up to 5 days per month
- 24-hour advance notice to manager
- No formal approval process needed

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Remote Work Eligibility:
- Role does not require on-site presence
- Demonstrated self-management and productivity
- Consistent performance ratings of "Meets Expectations" or above
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps download)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Required equipment and secure work environment

Ineligible Roles:
- Client-facing positions requiring physical presence
- Roles handling confidential physical documents
- Positions requiring specialized on-site equipment
- New hires during first 90 days (training period)

WORK HOURS AND AVAILABILITY

Core Hours: 10AM-3PM local time
- Must be available for meetings and collaboration
- Expected to respond to messages within 30 minutes

Flexible Hours:
- Complete 8-hour workday between 6AM-10PM
- Adjust start/end times with manager approval
- Record actual hours in time tracking system

Meeting Guidelines:
- Schedule meetings during core hours when possible
- Maximum 4 hours of meetings per day
- "No meeting" Fridays between 1-5PM

EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY

Company-Provided Equipment:
- Laptop computer
- Monitor (for full-time remote workers)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls
- Docking station (if needed)

Home Office Stipend:
- One-time: $500 for desk and ergonomic chair
- Monthly: $75 for internet reimbursement
- Annual: $200 for equipment upgrades

Required Software:
- VPN for secure connection
- Time tracking tool
- Collaboration platforms (Slack, Zoom, Microsoft Teams)
- Cloud storage access

Security Requirements:
- Use company VPN at all times
- Encrypted hard drive
- Screen privacy filter for public spaces
- Two-factor authentication enabled
- No public WiFi for accessing company systems

WORKSPACE REQUIREMENTS

Home Office Standards:
- Dedicated workspace separate from living areas
- Proper lighting and ergonomic setup
- Minimal background noise during calls
- Professional background for video meetings

Co-Working Spaces:
- Company will reimburse up to $300/month
- Must have secure, private workspaces
- Pre-approved locations only

Coffee Shops/Public Spaces:
- Acceptable for occasional work
- Not for handling sensitive information
- Use mobile hotspot instead of public WiFi

PERFORMANCE AND ACCOUNTABILITY

Productivity Expectations:
- Meet all deadlines and deliverables
- Maintain same performance standards as office work
- Complete weekly status updates
- Respond to communications promptly

Check-Ins:
- Daily standup meetings (15 minutes)
- Weekly one-on-ones with manager
- Monthly team sync (in-person or video)
- Quarterly performance reviews

Success Metrics:
- Quality of work output
- Meeting participation and engagement
- Responsiveness to communications
- Collaboration with team members

COLLABORATION TOOLS

Video Conferencing:
- Keep camera on during meetings
- Use virtual backgrounds if workspace not professional
- Mute when not speaking
- Test audio/video before important meetings

Instant Messaging:
- Set status to indicate availability
- Respond to urgent messages within 15 minutes
- Use "Do Not Disturb" for focused work (max 2 hours)

Document Collaboration:
- Store all files in cloud-based systems
- Use real-time collaboration features
- Maintain version control
- Share access appropriately

EXPENSES AND REIMBURSEMENT

Reimbursable Expenses:
- Internet service: Up to $75/month
- Mobile phone: Up to $50/month
- Printer supplies: Up to $25/month
- Office supplies: Up to $30/month

Non-Reimbursable:
- Rent or mortgage payments
- Utilities (except internet)
- Coffee or meals
- Personal phone calls

Submission Process:
- Submit receipts within 30 days
- Use expense management system
- Requires manager approval
- Reimbursement within 2 weeks

RETURN TO OFFICE PROTOCOL

Advance Notice:
- Company reserves right to require office presence
- Minimum 30 days notice for policy changes
- 24-hour notice for emergency situations

Transition Support:
- Commuter benefits reinst ated
- Parking validation provided
- Equipment return/exchange process
- Flexible transition period (30 days)

POLICY VIOLATIONS

Warning System:
- First violation: Verbal warning
- Second violation: Written warning
- Third violation: Remote privileges revoked
- Serious violations: Immediate revocation

Examples of Violations:
- Consistent unavailability during core hours
- Missing deadlines or deliverables
- Security breaches
- Not maintaining professional workspace
- Failure to attend required in-person meetings

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

Temporary Remote Work:
- Medical reasons: With doctor's note
- Caregiving responsibilities: Up to 30 days
- Weather emergencies: Manager discretion
- Home emergencies: Immediate approval

International Remote Work:
- Requires legal and tax review
- Advance approval needed (90 days minimum)
- Limited to 30 consecutive days
- Must maintain US work hours
