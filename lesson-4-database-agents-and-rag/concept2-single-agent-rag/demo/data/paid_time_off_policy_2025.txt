PAID TIME OFF (PTO) POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document Version: 2025.1

PTO ACCRUAL RATES

Employees accrue PTO based on length of service and employment status:

Years 0-2:
- Full-time: 15 days per year (1.25 days per month)
- Part-time (20+ hrs): 7.5 days per year

Years 3-5:
- Full-time: 20 days per year (1.67 days per month)
- Part-time (20+ hrs): 10 days per year

Years 6-10:
- Full-time: 25 days per year (2.08 days per month)
- Part-time (20+ hrs): 12.5 days per year

Years 11+:
- Full-time: 30 days per year (2.5 days per month)
- Part-time (20+ hrs): 15 days per year

ADDITIONAL PAID TIME OFF

Holidays: 10 paid company holidays per year
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day  
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

Sick Leave: Separate from PTO, 5 days per year (non-accruing)
- For personal illness or medical appointments
- May be used for immediate family member care
- Does not roll over to following year

PTO USAGE GUIDELINES

Minimum Request: Half-day (4 hours) increments
Advance Notice Requirements:
- 1-3 days: 1 week notice
- 4-10 days: 2 weeks notice
- 11+ days: 1 month notice

Blackout Periods:
- Last 2 weeks of fiscal quarter
- Major product launches (30-day notice provided)
- Year-end closing (December 15-31)

Maximum Consecutive Days: 15 business days without manager approval

CARRYOVER AND PAYOUT

Annual Carryover:
- Maximum 5 days may roll to next year
- Excess days forfeited on December 31
- Carryover days must be used by March 31

Separation Payout:
- Unused PTO paid out at final pay rate
- Minimum 2 weeks notice required for payout
- Termination for cause: no payout

REQUESTING PTO

Process:
1. Submit request through HR portal
2. Manager approval required within 3 business days
3. Confirmation email sent to employee
4. Calendar automatically updated

Emergency PTO:
- Contact manager directly
- Submit formal request within 24 hours
- Retroactive approval at manager discretion

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

Bereavement Leave: 3-5 days (not deducted from PTO)
- Immediate family: 5 days
- Extended family: 3 days

Jury Duty: Paid time off with proof of summons
Military Leave: Per USERRA requirements

Parental Leave:
- 12 weeks paid (primary caregiver)
- 6 weeks paid (secondary caregiver)
- Additional unpaid FMLA time available
