PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND TUITION REIMBURSEMENT
Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Document Version: 2025.1

TUITION REIMBURSEMENT PROGRAM

Eligibility Requirements:
- Minimum 6 months continuous employment
- Full-time or part-time (20+ hours/week) status
- Current performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher
- No active disciplinary actions

Reimbursement Amounts:

Undergraduate Degrees:
- Full-time employees: Up to $5,250 per calendar year
- Part-time employees: Up to $2,625 per calendar year
- Maximum: 2 courses per semester

Graduate Degrees (Master's, MBA, PhD):
- Full-time employees: Up to $10,000 per calendar year
- Part-time employees: Up to $5,000 per calendar year
- Maximum: 2 courses per semester

Professional Certifications:
- Up to $3,000 per year for job-related certifications
- Includes exam fees, study materials, and prep courses
- Examples: PMP, CPA, SHRM-CP, AWS, Cisco, Six Sigma

Individual Courses (Non-Degree):
- Up to $2,000 per year
- Must be directly related to current role or career path within company

Grade Requirements:
- Undergraduate: Minimum grade of "C" or 2.0 GPA
- Graduate: Minimum grade of "B" or 3.0 GPA
- Certification: Must pass exam on first or second attempt
- Pass/Fail courses: Must receive "Pass"

REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES

Covered Costs:
- Tuition and mandatory fees
- Required textbooks and course materials
- Lab fees and software
- Certification exam fees (2 attempts maximum)
- Application fees (if enrolled)

Not Covered:
- Parking or transportation
- Meals or lodging
- Late fees or penalties
- Non-required materials
- Courses dropped after add/drop period

PRE-APPROVAL PROCESS

Application Steps:
1. Complete Tuition Reimbursement Application 45 days before course start
2. Obtain manager approval and HR signature
3. Submit course catalog description and syllabus
4. Demonstrate relevance to current role or career path
5. Receive written approval before registering

Required Documentation:
- Official course description
- Cost breakdown and payment schedule
- Academic calendar with key dates
- Proof of accreditation for institution

Approval Timeline:
- Application review: 10 business days
- Manager approval: 5 business days
- HR final approval: 5 business days
- Total process: Approximately 20 business days

REIMBURSEMENT PROCESS

Submission Requirements:
- Submit within 60 days of course completion
- Provide official transcript or grade report
- Include itemized receipt of all expenses
- Attach proof of payment

Payment Timeline:
- Reimbursement processed within 30 days of submission
- Direct deposit to employee's payroll account
- Taxable as income per IRS regulations

REPAYMENT OBLIGATION

Service Commitment:
Course completed within last 12 months: Repay 100% if leave company
Course completed 12-24 months ago: Repay 50% if leave company
Course completed 24+ months ago: No repayment required

Exceptions to Repayment:
- Termination without cause
- Layoff or position elimination
- Disability preventing work
- Retirement (age 55+ with 10 years service)

Repayment Terms:
- Due within 30 days of separation
- Deducted from final paycheck if applicable
- Payment plan available for amounts over $5,000

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT BUDGET

Annual Allowance:
- All employees: $1,500 per year for professional development
- Does not count toward tuition reimbursement limits
- Use-it-or-lose-it (no carryover)

Eligible Activities:
- Industry conferences and workshops
- Professional association memberships
- Online learning platforms (LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, Udemy)
- Books and publications related to field
- Webinars and virtual training sessions

Request Process:
- Manager approval required
- Book through company learning portal
- Automatic reimbursement (no grade requirement)

INTERNAL TRAINING PROGRAMS

Leadership Development:
- Emerging Leaders Program (high potentials, 6-month cohort)
- Manager Training Series (new managers, quarterly)
- Executive Coaching (Director+, by nomination)

Technical Skills:
- Software and tools training (free, self-paced)
- Industry certifications (company-sponsored)
- Lunch and learns (weekly sessions)

Soft Skills:
- Communication and presentation skills
- Project management fundamentals
- Time management and productivity
- Conflict resolution

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM

Formal Mentorship:
- 6-month or 12-month programs
- Matched based on career goals
- Monthly meetings required
- Development plan created

Reverse Mentorship:
- Junior employees mentor senior leaders
- Focus on technology, trends, diverse perspectives
- 3-month program cycles

Cross-Functional Mentorship:
- Learn about different departments
- Job shadowing opportunities
- Quarterly rotations available

CAREER PATHING SUPPORT

Career Development Meetings:
- Annual career planning session with manager
- Individual Development Plan (IDP) created
- Quarterly check-ins on progress

Internal Job Rotation:
- 3-6 month assignments in different departments
- Maintain current salary and benefits
- Approval required from both managers

Stretch Assignments:
- Special projects outside current role
- Build new skills and visibility
- No permanent transfer required

SABBATICAL PROGRAM

Eligibility:
- 7+ years of continuous service
- Strong performance history
- Manager and executive approval

Sabbatical Options:
- 1 month partially paid (50% salary)
- 2 months unpaid
- 3 months unpaid (for special circumstances)

Benefits During Sabbatical:
- Health insurance maintained
- 401(k) vesting continues
- Position held for duration
- No PTO accrual during unpaid leave

LANGUAGE LEARNING

Business Travel Languages:

CONFERENCE ATTENDANCE

Professional Conferences:
- 1-2 conferences per year based on role
- Company pays registration, travel, lodging

- Must share learnings with team upon return

Industry Certifications at Conferences:
- Testing fees covered if taken at conference
- Study materials reimbursed

LEARNING TIME POLICY

Dedicated Learning Hours:
- 5 hours per month for self-directed learning
- Use during work hours (with manager agreement)
- Document in learning management system

Friday Learning Sessions:
- Last Friday of month: 2PM-5PM
- Team learning activities
- Guest speakers and workshops
- Optional but highly encouraged

POLICY COMPLIANCE

Annual Limits:
- Cannot combine tuition + professional development beyond stated maximums
- Manager discretion for exceptional cases
- CFO approval required for amounts over annual limits

Conflict of Interest:
- Cannot use degree/certification to immediately leave for competitor
- Non-compete agreements may apply
- Discuss career intentions openly with manager
