Overdraft & NSF Policy (Rev. Apr 2025)
Effective Date: April 15, 2025

1. OVERDRAFT PROTECTION OVERVIEW
1.1 Standard Overdraft Coverage:
    - Automatically covers checks, ACH transactions, and automatic bill payments
    - Does NOT cover ATM and debit card transactions unless opted in
    - Available for accounts open 30+ days with regular direct deposits

1.2 Opt-In ATM/Debit Coverage:
    - Customer must explicitly opt-in for ATM and debit card overdraft coverage
    - Can be enabled online, by phone, or in-branch
    - Can be disabled at any time without penalty

2. OVERDRAFT FEES AND LIMITS
2.1 Overdraft Fee: $35.00 per occurrence
2.2 Maximum Overdraft Fees: 
    - 4 fees per day (maximum $140.00 daily)
    - No more than 8 fees per monthly statement cycle

2.3 NSF (Non-Sufficient Funds) Fee: $35.00 per occurrence
2.4 Maximum NSF Fees: 3 fees per day (maximum $105.00 daily)

3. OVERDRAFT FEE WAIVERS
3.1 Automatic Waivers:
    - Overdrafts of $5.00 or less: No fee charged
    - Account brought positive within 24 hours: Fee waived
    - First overdraft in 12 months: Fee waived for customers in good standing

3.2 Grace Period:
    - 24-hour grace period to deposit funds before fee assessment
    - Grace period starts at end of business day when overdraft occurs
    - Applies to first overdraft incident per day only

4. OVERDRAFT PROTECTION TRANSFER SERVICES
4.1 Savings Account Link:
    - Transfer fee: $10.00 per transfer
    - Minimum transfer: $100.00 or exact overdraft amount
    - Available for overdrafts up to available savings balance

4.2 Line of Credit Link:
    - Transfer fee: $10.00 per transfer
    - Interest charges apply to outstanding balance
    - Credit approval required

4.3 Credit Card Link:
    - Cash advance fee: 4% of transfer amount (minimum $10.00)
    - Credit card interest rates apply
    - Subject to available credit limit

5. ACCOUNT CLOSURE POLICY
5.1 Extended Overdraft:
    - Accounts negative for 60+ consecutive days may be closed
    - Customer remains liable for negative balance plus fees
    - May be reported to ChexSystems

5.2 Excessive Overdraft Activity:
    - More than 6 overdrafts in any 6-month period
    - Account may be restricted or closed at bank's discretion
    - Alternative account products may be offered

6. CUSTOMER RIGHTS AND NOTIFICATIONS
6.1 Real-Time Alerts:
    - Text/email alerts for low balances (customizable thresholds)
    - Overdraft notifications sent immediately
    - Daily balance updates available

6.2 Opt-Out Rights:
    - Customers can opt-out of overdraft coverage at any time
    - Opt-out applies to future transactions only
    - ATM/debit transactions will be declined if opted out
