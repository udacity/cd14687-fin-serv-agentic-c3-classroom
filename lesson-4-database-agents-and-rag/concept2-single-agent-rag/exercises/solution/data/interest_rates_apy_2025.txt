Interest Tiers & APY Disclosures (Savings/MMA)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. STATEMENT SAVINGS ACCOUNT
1.1 Interest Rate Tiers:
    - $0 - $2,499: 0.01% APY
    - $2,500 - $9,999: 0.05% APY  
    - $10,000 - $24,999: 0.15% APY
    - $25,000 - $99,999: 0.25% APY
    - $100,000+: 0.35% APY

1.2 Interest Calculation:
    - Compounded daily
    - Credited monthly on statement date
    - Minimum balance to earn interest: $100
    - Average daily balance method

2. MONEY MARKET ACCOUNT (MMA)
2.1 Interest Rate Tiers:
    - $0 - $9,999: 0.10% APY
    - $10,000 - $24,999: 0.35% APY
    - $25,000 - $49,999: 0.75% APY
    - $50,000 - $99,999: 1.25% APY
    - $100,000 - $249,999: 1.75% APY
    - $250,000+: 2.25% APY

2.2 Account Requirements:
    - Minimum opening deposit: $2,500
    - Minimum balance to avoid fee: $2,500
    - Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with minimum balance)

3. PREMIER SAVINGS ACCOUNT
3.1 Enhanced Interest Rates:
    - $0 - $9,999: 0.15% APY
    - $10,000 - $49,999: 0.50% APY
    - $50,000 - $99,999: 1.00% APY
    - $100,000 - $249,999: 1.50% APY
    - $250,000+: 2.00% APY

3.2 Premier Requirements:
    - Combined relationship balance of $25,000+
    - Includes checking, savings, CDs, and investment accounts
    - Premier checking account required

4. CERTIFICATE OF DEPOSIT (CD) RATES
4.1 Standard CD Terms and Rates:
    - 3 months: 0.25% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 6 months: 0.50% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 12 months: 1.25% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 18 months: 1.75% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 24 months: 2.25% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 36 months: 2.75% APY (minimum $1,000)
    - 60 months: 3.25% APY (minimum $1,000)

4.2 Jumbo CD Rates ($100,000 minimum):
    - Add 0.25% to standard rates for all terms
    - Special promotional rates may apply periodically

5. INTEREST RATE DISCLOSURES
5.1 Rate Change Policy:
    - Variable rates may change at any time
    - 30-day notice for rate decreases (savings/MMA)
    - CD rates fixed for entire term
    - Rate changes effective on statement cycle date

5.2 Penalty Disclosures:
    - Early CD withdrawal penalty: 90 days interest (terms under 2 years)
    - Early CD withdrawal penalty: 180 days interest (terms 2+ years)
    - Minimum penalty: $25 regardless of interest earned

6. FEDERAL REGULATIONS
6.1 Regulation D (Pre-authorized Transfer Limits):
    - Savings and MMA: 6 transfers per month limit removed (effective 2020)
    - Excessive activity monitoring may still apply
    - Bank reserves right to convert to checking if needed

6.2 FDIC Insurance:
    - All deposits insured up to $250,000 per depositor, per bank
    - Interest-bearing and non-interest-bearing deposits combined
    - Separate coverage for different ownership categories

7. MINIMUM BALANCE REQUIREMENTS
7.1 To Open Account:
    - Statement Savings: $25
    - Money Market: $2,500
    - Premier Savings: $100 (with qualifying relationship)
    - CDs: $1,000 (standard), $100,000 (jumbo)

7.2 To Avoid Monthly Fees:
    - Statement Savings: $300 daily balance
    - Money Market: $2,500 daily balance  
    - Premier Savings: No monthly fee
    - CDs: No monthly fees during term
