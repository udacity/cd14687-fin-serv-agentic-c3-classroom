Personal Checking Fee Schedule (2025)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. ACCOUNT MAINTENANCE FEES
1.1 Monthly Maintenance Fee: $12.00
1.2 Waiver Conditions: 
    - Maintain minimum daily balance of $1,500, OR
    - Direct deposit of $500+ per month, OR
    - Age 65+ with valid ID

2. TRANSACTION FEES
2.1 Overdraft Fee: $35.00 per occurrence
2.2 NSF (Non-Sufficient Funds) Fee: $35.00 per occurrence
2.3 Overdraft Protection Transfer: $10.00 per transfer
2.4 Stop Payment Fee: $30.00

3. ATM AND DEBIT CARD FEES
3.1 Out-of-Network ATM Fee: $2.50 per transaction
3.2 International ATM Fee: $5.00 per transaction
3.3 ATM Fee Reimbursements: None for standard checking
3.4 Card Replacement Fee: $5.00 for expedited delivery

4. OTHER FEES
4.1 Paper Statement Fee: $3.00 per month
4.2 Check Printing: Varies by style ($15-45 per box)
4.3 Cashier's Check: $10.00
4.4 Money Order: $5.00

Premier Checking Benefits:
- No monthly maintenance fee with $2,500 minimum balance
- Up to 6 out-of-network ATM fee reimbursements per month
- Free cashier's checks and money orders
- Free expedited card replacement
