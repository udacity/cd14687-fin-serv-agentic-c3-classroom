Mobile Deposit Limits & Hold Periods (Consumer)
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. MOBILE DEPOSIT ELIGIBILITY
1.1 Account Requirements:
    - Account open for 30+ days
    - No returned deposits in previous 6 months
    - Mobile banking app enrollment required
    - Valid debit card on file

1.2 Check Requirements:
    - Personal checks, business checks, government checks only
    - U.S. currency only
    - Checks must be endorsed "For mobile deposit only"
    - Maximum age: 60 days from date on check

2. DEPOSIT LIMITS
2.1 New Customers (Accounts 0-90 days):
    - Daily limit: $500
    - Monthly limit: $2,500
    - Single check limit: $500

2.2 Established Customers (Accounts 90+ days):
    - Daily limit: $5,000
    - Monthly limit: $25,000
    - Single check limit: $5,000

2.3 Premier Account Holders:
    - Daily limit: $10,000
    - Monthly limit: $50,000
    - Single check limit: $10,000

2.4 Business Account Limits:
    - Daily limit: $25,000 (or as individually approved)
    - Monthly limit: $100,000
    - Single check limit: $25,000

3. FUNDS AVAILABILITY SCHEDULE
3.1 Standard Hold Periods:
    - Personal checks: 1 business day for first $225, remainder on 2nd business day
    - Government checks: Next business day availability
    - Cashier's checks: Next business day availability

3.2 New Account Hold Periods (First 30 days):
    - All deposits: Up to 7 business days
    - First $5,000 may be available next business day
    - Applies to accounts open less than 30 days

3.3 Extended Hold Conditions:
    - Large deposits over $5,000: Additional 1-2 business days
    - Redeposited checks: Up to 7 business days
    - Reasonable doubt about collectibility: Up to 7 business days

4. CUT-OFF TIMES
4.1 Same-Day Processing:
    - Monday-Friday before 6:00 PM ET
    - Saturday before 12:00 PM ET
    - No Sunday processing

4.2 Next-Day Processing:
    - Deposits after cut-off times
    - Federal holidays (processed next business day)
    - System maintenance windows

5. PROHIBITED TRANSACTIONS
5.1 Ineligible Check Types:
    - Money orders
    - Cashier's checks over $5,000 (security requirement)
    - Foreign checks or foreign currency
    - Checks payable to third parties
    - Post-dated checks
    - Stale-dated checks (over 6 months old)

5.2 Quality Requirements:
    - All four corners of check must be visible
    - No tears, stains, or alterations
    - Clear, legible images required
    - Both front and back images required

6. EXCEPTION HANDLING
6.1 Deposit Rejection Reasons:
    - Poor image quality
    - Duplicate deposit detection
    - Insufficient account history
    - Suspected fraud indicators

6.2 Funds Recovery:
    - Bank may reverse mobile deposits if checks are returned
    - Customer liable for returned check fees
    - Negative balances may result from reversals
    - Multiple returns may result in mobile deposit suspension
